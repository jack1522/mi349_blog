Who is Kim?

Hello! I am Kim , the author of this blog of course! I am currently a Junior at Michigan State University. I am studying Computer Science with hopes to become a Software Engineer one day. Here At MSU, I am involved involved in two student orginizations. The first one is the National Society of Black Engineers aka NSBE. The second one is Women in Engineering aka WIE. In WIE, I hold a leadership role as a mentor where I mentor a freshman woman who is entering the STEM field. I plan to get more involved on campus as this school year progresses.

Aside from my academic studies, there is much more to know about me. I have aspirations to travel the world. I would like to travel to all 50 states along with many countries. This is a goal that I would like to reach by the time that I am 30. Other interesting facts about me include my passion for creating music and designing clothing pieces for myself.
